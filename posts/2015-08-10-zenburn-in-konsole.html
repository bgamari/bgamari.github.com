Installing ZenBurn

2015-08-10 | tagged with
  • konsole

Recently I got stuck for quite a while trying to use the Zenburn colorscheme in konsole. While there are a number of sources for konsole .colorscheme files for [Zenburn][] and other themes, it isn’t entirely clear where these files belong. Some sources claim ~/.kde/share/apps/konsole, others claim ~/.kde4/share/apps/konsole. Naturally neither of these options worked for me on Debian testing.

It seems that Konsole now looks in ~/.local/share/konsole for its themes.
